1. Description
Part of making white vinegar (dilute acetic acid) for separation of calcium carbonates and to make acetate salts.

2. Inputs
None

3. Outputs
Apples

4. Procedure
    1. Pinch the stem of fully grown apple on the apple tree.
    2. Pull and twist to pick the apple from the tree.

5. Yield
Unknowable. Yield of apples from trees will vary by tree.

6. Time Cost
1 min/kg
