1. Description
Forms the apple cider base to make apple cider vinegar to make white vinegar.

2. Inputs
Apple chunks

3. Outputs
Apple pomace; Apple cider

4. Procedure
    1. Using a consumer grating-type juicer, juice the apple chunks according to the manufacturer directions, hence separating the apple pomace from the juice.

5. Yield
0.60±0.05 mL/g of cider/apple chunks. Equivalently, 0.63±0.05 g/g of cider/apple chunks.

6. Time Cost
13 min/kg
