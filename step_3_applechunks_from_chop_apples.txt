1. Description
Turns apples into smaller chunks for easier juicing to make vinegar.

2. Inputs
Apples

3. Outputs
Apple chunks

4. Procedure
    1. Cut each apple with a knife in half along Cartesian x, y, and z axes, thereby creating 8 pieces roughly equal in volume.

5. Yield
100% (no mass or volume lost)

6. Time Cost
8 min/kg
